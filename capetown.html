Cape Town City

Cape Town is one of South Africa's most cosmopolitan and vibrant cities. A city blessed with natural beauty in spades. Cape Town compliments its many blessings from mother nature by being a diverse and fascinating city on its own right. There are so many tourist attractions that visitors can go and see. From the heights of Table Mountain to the dramatic stretches of the coastline. One can enjoy themselves in the cable car to the mountain.The beaches in Cape Town are so beautiful that you will wish you stay there forever. From Clifton Beach to the penguin playground of Boulders Beach, there are so many options for those wanting to work on their tans and have a little fun in the sun. South Africa's apartheid musuem Robben Island is also situated in these city. Its also a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Kirstenbosch Botanical Gardens, boasting as "the most beautiful garden in Africa" definetely has a right to claim. Cape Town is a definetely a remarkable place. You will never regret your decision.
