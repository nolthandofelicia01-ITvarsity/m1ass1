Edinburgh City

One of the most beautiful cities in all of the UK. Edinburgh rises from the wide Firth of Forth to a high, rocky pinnacle crowned by the stone walls and towers of Edinburgh Castle. The Scottish capital is a center of culture and the arts,and is especially well-known for its festivals. These include the Edinburgh International Book festival, which welcomes more than 1000 authores to the sparkling Christmas Markets. Edinburgh is famous for its seat of the Scottish Government, the Scottish Parliament and the supreme courts of Scotland. There are also top sights in Edinburgh for tourists like the Edinburgh Castle with spectacular old fortress with city views, Palace of Holyroodhouse and Holyrood Abbey and .The Royal Mile. In these city one can relax and view all the history of the city. There is also the Arthur's Seat and the Salisbury Crags at 820 feet, it is the highest point inthe 640-acre Holyrood Park. The spectacular views from the top encompass the whole city all the the way to the mouth of the Forth. Then we have the Royal Yacht Bitannia which is one of the most popular attractions associated with the monarchy. Most people love the Royal Botanic Garden Edinburgh (RBGE), its the second oldest such garden in Britain. Learn the history about this place and see how they practise their culture.
