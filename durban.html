Durban City

Durban, otherwise known as Ethekwini, is recognised as the largest city in KwaZulu-Natal and is famous for being the busiest port in South Africa. We all know Durban for the warmth, subtropical weather and vast, amazing beaches, which makes it a trophical paradise for lazy, relaxing days in the sun and exciting nights filled with bright lights, music and busy streets filled with people and an energy that is exciting yet can't quite be defined. When we talk about fun, we are talking about Durban. Durban also offers tourists a chance to sightsee whilst enjoying themselves. You can start your day at the Isandlwana Battlefields, where the South African and British Colonial history was made in a major battle. You will get a chance to learn about the great battle Zulu warriors and the British troops in 1879. One can also go to the Drakensburg Mountain on a day trip to Durban. Take in the endless vistas and incredible scenery. Not to mention the Beaches in Durban, you'll never want to go back home. Durban has different beaches such as Umhlanga beach, famous for its red and white lighthouse,long stretches of light sand, warm waters and picturesquare backdrops. Then we have Thompsons Bay Beach, Sadwana Bay, Scottburgh beach and Umdloti Beach, which offers fun and surely you will have a great day. Durban also offers you fun again at the Suncoast Casino. You will never get bored while being in Durban thats for sure. The cultures are also amazing and the diferent food types are to die for.
